Help with separation and divorce

Marriages and relationships sometimes end in divorce or separation. If you have children, it’s vital that your break-up goes as smoothly as possible to minimise the impact on them. Even if you don’t have kids, any relationship break down can affect other areas of your life. 

Here you'll find support and guidance to help you make this difficult transition easier for you and your family.

Are you ready to separate?

You can discuss your current situation with a trained counsellor in a Live Chat. It’s free, confidential and completely anonymous.

The opening hours are Tuesdays 6pm-8pm, Wednesdays 12-2pm and Sundays 7pm-9pm.
